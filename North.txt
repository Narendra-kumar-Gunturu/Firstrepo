North Korea and Cuba are the only places you can't buy Coca-Cola.<br>
No matter where you go, it's comforting to know you can always enjoy a Coca-Cola. Well, almost anywhere.<br>
While this fizzy drink is sold practically everywhere, it still hasn't  <br>
(officially) made its way to North Korea or Cuba, according to the BBC. <br>
 That's because these countries are under long-term U.S. trade embargoes.
